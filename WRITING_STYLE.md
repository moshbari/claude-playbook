# Writing style

_Last updated: 2026-04-18_

## The rule

Use 5th-grade reading level in **everything** — product copy, UI text, emails, chat replies, even internal notes.

## Why

My audience is 35+ folks looking for a 9-to-5 replacement side hustle. The test: "can an 80-year-old grandma with thick glasses read this?" If the answer is no, they close the tab and move on. Big words and jargon push them away.

## How to apply

- Short sentences. Usually 10 words or less.
- Simple words. No jargon. No "leverage," "optimize," "seamless," "robust."
- One idea per sentence.
- Read it back. If it sounds like a textbook, rewrite it like I'm telling a friend.
- This applies to chat replies to me too, not just the product.

## Checklist before shipping copy

- [ ] Every sentence fits on one line?
- [ ] Any word a 10-year-old would need a dictionary for? Replace it.
- [ ] Passive voice removed?
- [ ] Bullet points are short phrases, not paragraphs?
- [ ] Read aloud — does it sound like a friend or a robot?
